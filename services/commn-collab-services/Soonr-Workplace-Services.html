Every organisation faces a challenge of how to create and share documents securely and effectively. Its more important than ever to ensure that the correct version is being used and easily easily retrievable from any anywhere.

With Soonr Workplace, you can safely manage, organise, and share files with your Team and your Connections—people who work outside your company—from any device, anywhere.
