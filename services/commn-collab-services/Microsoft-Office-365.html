Microsoft Office 365


Overview

  • - Service: Microsoft Office 365
  • - Function: A combination of Microsoft Desktop Apps (Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Outlook, OneNote and Publisher) with web-enabled tools such as Exchange Email, Skype for Business (also known as Lync), OneDrive, SharePoint Online and others
  • - Commercial model: Monthly or yearly subscription with monthly invoicing

Description

Microsoft Office 365 is a subscription service that combines Microsoft Desktop Apps (Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Outlook, OneNote and Publisher) with web-enabled tools such as Exchange Email, Skype for Business (also known as Lync), OneDrive, SharePoint online and others. 

It also features Office on Demand – streaming live versions of your productivity tools directly to any computer so that you may access your Microsoft applications and tools from any point in the world. With Office 365 you don't waste time and resources on upgrading and patching – everything is automated. You'll always be working with the latest software version of Office 365.

Office 365 Business Family

The Business Family packages offer is suitable for organisations with up to 300 users and provides you with all the familiar Microsoft Office applications covering the core Office functionalities and comes in three versions:

  • - Office 365 Business includes Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, OneNote, and Publisher. This plan provides the full Office experience across PCs, Macs, Windows tablets and iPad and Android.
  • - Office 365 Business Essentials has the online version of Office with email and conferencing. The online version of Office includes Word, Excel and PowerPoint. It also comes with the standard online Email and calendars, SharePoint online, Skype for Business and Yammer – the enterprise social network.
  • - Office 365 Business Premium is the ultimate combination between the Business & Business Essentials packages.

Office Enterprise Family

The Enterprise Family packages are for larger organisations with no user limitation. It provides you with all the familiar Microsoft applications with the addition of many advanced features. The package comes in three versions:

  • - Office 365 ProPlus includes all the core Office applications, Access database, support for hybrid deployment and shared computer activation. Office 365 ProPlus enables a fully installed Office experience across PCs, Macs, Windows tablets and iPad and Android.
  • - Office 365 Enterprise E1 is the advanced and enterprise version of Office 365 Business Essentials. In addition to Pro Plus, you get support for hybrid development and Office 365 Video.
  • - Office 365 Enterprise E3 is the ultimate combination between ProPlus and E1 and, in addition, provides you with advanced compliance features such as email archiving, eDiscovery, and mailbox hold. It also comes with some advanced information protection features such as message encryption, Rights Management Service (RMS) and Data Loss Prevention (DLP).

Stand Alone Exchange Online Plans

Exchange online is a stand-alone service if you are only interested of getting a secure, reliable, and easy to implement email solution that is very easy to use and maintain. Exchange online comes in two versions:

  • - Exchange Online Plan 1 has 50 GB mailbox space per user, Outlook support, apps for your Outlook client, Web-based access, mobile access, shared calendars and contacts, Exchange online protection, In-Place Archive.
  • - Exchange Online Plan 2 adds up advanced functionalities to the features listed above with unlimited storage for your In-Place Archive, hosted voicemail and Data Loss Prevention (DLP).
