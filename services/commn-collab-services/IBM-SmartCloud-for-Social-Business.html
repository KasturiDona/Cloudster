Description

Buying and deploying an IBM enterprise-grade service has historically been beyond reach for many businesses. But alongside our data protection service powered by IBM’s Tivoli Storage Manager, Cloudster has simplified buying IBM solutions to enable deployment in minute not months.

IBM has integrated the essential tools for social business in the cloud: enterprise-grade file sharing, communities, instant messaging, web meetings, user profiles, mail, and calendar. The solution is designed to let you work seamlessly with people inside and outside of your organisation. You can meet online, share files, chat, manage projects, network with potential clients, schedule meetings, and send and receive mail anywhere, anytime. Whether you work remotely, manage remote teams, or need one place to bring colleagues, partners, and vendors together, This solution helps you transform your business into a social business.
