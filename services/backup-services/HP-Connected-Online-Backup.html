Description

Connected is the Enterprise grade PC and Laptop backup solution that does exactly what it says. Million users rely on HP Connected Backup for fully automatic, secure, efficient, and reliable backup and recovery of mission-critical data, no matter where it resides. HP Connected Backup is user-friendly and flexible, providing security, performance and great management tools:

  • - Data encrypted to 128-bit AES standard and uses SSL encryption when transferring it across the net – military-grade security levels.
  • - Daily backups take minutes to complete – only data that has changed since the last backup is saved and a new version created.
  • - Recover lost data immediately through the backup software – select the files you need back and click ‘Retrieve.
  • - MyRoam remote web portal feature gives you anytime, anywhere, access to your important files – recover your data using a standard web-browser from anywhere in the world.
  • - When recovering data choose from ten previous files versions stored in the vaults – guarantees you recover a good copy of data.
