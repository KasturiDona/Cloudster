About Us

Cloudster gives businesses the opportunity of having the optimal Unified Cloud Experience

Cloudster brings many of the best cloud-based IT services you need, together into one single unified experience. We have a mission of simplifying and removing all obstacles associated with procuring, deploying and consuming cloud in your business. We want to work alongside whilst providing you with the maximum IT functionality that you will love.
With Cloudster, our Cloud Management Platform and tools allows businesses to take control of all their cloud solutions. We allow businesses to know who is using what, what value is being created and, where your data is. There is one contract dashboard, one bill and one number to call for support.
